Scooter Rides and Random Thoughts 🛴

There’s something magical about riding my scooter through the park. It’s not just transport, it’s therapy on wheels. The rhythm of pushing forward, the little breeze in my face, and the playlist blasting through my headphones it all adds up to a moving diary session.

I think about everything and nothing at the same time. Politics, random jokes, blog ideas, or even what snack I’m craving next. It’s chaotic, but in the best way. Sometimes, it feels like the scooter takes my thoughts out for a walk too.

But let’s be real half the time I’m also scared of knocking over a dog, swerving into a kid on a tricycle, or worse… flying off and losing all my front teeth. May that never happen 🙏. Still, I keep riding, because the joy outweighs the fear.
